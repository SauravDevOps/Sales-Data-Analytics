Analysis Steps:
1.	Load the dataset using pandas
2.	Convert Date column to datetime
3.	Handle missing values using fillna()
4.	Remove duplicates using drop duplicates()
5.	Create Revenue, Cost, and Profit columns
6.	Calculate:
-	Total Sales
-	Total Profit
-	Profit Margin 
7.	Group data by Month to analyse trends
8.	Group data by Product to identify the Top 3 products
9.	Generate visualisations

Key Findings:
1. Revenue Insights
•	Total Revenue indicates overall business performance.
•	High-priced products contribute significantly to revenue even if quantity sold is lower.
2. Best Performing Products
•	The Top 3 products contribute most of the total revenue.
•	Premium products (like laptops) generate higher revenue despite lower volume.
3. Monthly Sales Trend
•	Sales show variation across months.
•	Certain months demonstrate stronger revenue performance, indicating potential seasonality.
4. Profitability Analysis
•	Total Profit was calculated after estimating cost.
•	Profit Margin (%) indicates business efficiency.
•	Higher revenue does not always guarantee higher profit if costs are high.
5. Business Insight
•	Revenue concentration suggests dependency on specific products.
•	Diversifying product sales could reduce business risk.
•	Monitoring monthly trends can help in inventory and marketing planning.
